§ IIWHAT I WORK ON

Research themes

01

Medical Vision Models

Grounded reasoning, segmentation, and report generation across X-ray, CT, ultrasound, and MRI. Building datasets and benchmarks that hold models accountable to clinical truth.

  • → 3DReasonKnee
  • → ReXGroundingCT
  • → FactCheXcker
  • → ReXSonoVQA
02

Dynamic AI Evaluation

Static benchmarks miss how AI behaves in the wild. We build simulators and clinically grounded metrics so models are evaluated the way they are deployed.

  • → Clinical Environment Simulator (Nature Medicine)
  • → CRIMSON
  • → ReXTrust
03

LLMs in the Clinic

Multi-agent diagnosis, voice agents for prehospital care, and the under-studied failure modes of chatty models — sycophancy, hallucination, premature closure.

  • → VOICE (IEEE AIxMHC)
  • → Sycophancy in Medical LLMs
  • → Mixed-Vendor Diagnosis
04

Translational Orthopedic AI

Deployed deep-learning pipelines for alignment measurement, fracture detection, and outcome prediction — validated across institutions and now used in real clinical workflows.

  • → Hip Fracture DL
  • → Automated Tibial Slope
  • → CPAK Analysis (JBJS)
